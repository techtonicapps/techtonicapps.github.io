Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how we handle user data and ensure their privacy when using Autonix.

1. Data Collection

Our app does not collect any personal data from users. We respect your privacy and believe in transparency.

2. Types of Data We Don't Collect

  • Personal Information: We don't collect names, addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • Device Information: We don't track your device's unique identifiers (e.g., IMEI, MAC address).
  • Location Data: We don't access your location.
  • Usage Data: We don't monitor your app usage or interactions.

3. Permissions

Our app requests only the necessary permissions to function properly. These permissions include Location Access, Bluetooth Connection Details and Post Notification.

4. Third Parties

We don't share any user data with third parties. Our app operates entirely on your device.

5. Security

We take security seriously. Our app is designed to work offline, minimizing any potential risks.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

If we ever update our privacy policy, we'll notify you within the app or through other communication channels.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, feel free to contact us at techtonicapplications@gmail.com.
